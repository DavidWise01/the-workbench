The Workbenchone conversation in — the whole pipeline, end to end

Every sphere of the Eskimo Brothers stack demoed one stage. This is all of them in one tool. Drop a raw multi-party conversation and a directory of who-has-what-authority, and the Workbench runs the entire pipeline live: it screens the noise, classifies each line by speech-act and tone, builds the validated substrate (the typed causal DAG), projects it to both faithful exits — HACI-C the document and MACI the message stream — and adjudicates by authority into a certified decision. Deterministic, in-browser, no model in the loop.
